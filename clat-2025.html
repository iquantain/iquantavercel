Understanding CLAT 2025

CLAT 2025 Overview: CLAT is a national level law entrance exam conducted for admissions into undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) law programs offered by 22 National Law Universities in India.

Significance of CLAT: It is the gateway to prestigious law schools and a career in legal professions. Excelling in CLAT opens doors to top-tier universities and numerous opportunities in the legal field.
